Areas of action

ATTRACT

Attracting international founding teams and startups to the entrepreneurial and research hub in central Germany

  • International business matchmaking events
  • Connecting EXIST teams with international IT specialists
  • Attracting companies and startups to relocate to the region
  • Born global startup events

ACADEMY

Developing competencies for company growth and internationalization

  • Over 30 training courses on topics such as design thinking, lean startup, customer journey, coachings with experts
  • E-learning from any location
  • Extensive workshops on early-stage financing and sales training
  • Preparing investor pitches and individual consultations

ACCELERATE

Supporting companies entering the Asian market through hubs in China, Japan and Vietnam

  • Rapid integration into the entrepreneurial scene in the target market
  • Attractive local location
  • Events and workshops
  • Finding and retaining business partners
  • Developing a local business network in the target market

About Us

For the project “International Startup Campus” Martin-Luther-University Halle-Wittenberg, Friedrich Schiller University Jena and Leipzig University are collaborating to support startups and business founding teams by connecting these with international founding members/entrepreneurs and supporting their international market entry. This project is supported by the German Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy.
